Kinetics

Kinetics is the area of chemistry concerning reaction rates. This is difference from spontaneity, the inherent tendency for a reaction to occur, and from thermodynamics, concerning the energy of reactions. Many spontaneous reactions occur so slowly that the changes are not detectable. Likewise, reactions that release large amounts of energy are not necessarily slower or faster than those that absorb energy or release only a small amount of energy. For reactions to be useful, they must be reasonably fast. Kinetics can help manufacturers speed up reactions.
