Reaction Mechanism

A reaction mechanism is the series of elementary steps of a reaction.

For example, the reaction mechanism of a fictional reaction A2 + 2B -> 2AB is
A2 -> A + A
A + B -> AB

Elementary Step Molecularity Rate Law
A -> products Unimolecular Rate=k[A]
A+A -> products Bimolecular Rate=k[A]2
A+B -> products Bimolecular Rate=k[A][B]
A+A+A -> products Termolecular Rate=k[A]3
A+A+B -> products Termolecular Rate=k[A]2[B]
A+B+C -> products Termolecular Rate=k[A][B][C]
