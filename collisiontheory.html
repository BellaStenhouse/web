4. Collision Theory

For reactants to collide successfully to rearrange to form products:

  1. The collision energy must equal or exceed the activation energy.
  2. The relative orientation of the reactants must allow formation of any new bonds necessary to produce products.

The collision theory was first addressed by Svante Arrhenius. He's a cool guy!
